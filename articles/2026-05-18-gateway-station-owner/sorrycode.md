Beginner-first action path:

1. Check whether the platform distinguishes tool, model, gateway, and upstream.
2. Ask for model mapping.
3. Ask what "compatible" guarantees and what it does not.
4. Check whether usage breakdown includes input, output, cache, retry, and
   failure behavior.
5. Check how balance deduction maps to token price, multiplier, and failed
   requests.

Do not expose SorryCode internal routing or private provider details.
