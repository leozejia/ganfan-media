# Articles

Each article or campaign gets its own folder:

```text
YYYY-MM-DD-topic-slug/
├── brief.md
├── sources.md
├── x.md
├── sorrycode.md
├── assets/
├── outputs/
└── publish.md
```

Use `brief.md` to define the audience, angle, and factual boundary. Keep source material in `sources.md`. Put final channel drafts in `x.md` and `sorrycode.md`. Put rendered channel artifacts under `outputs/`. Record shipped links and feedback in `publish.md`.
